NIA AI Training Dataset Construction Project

Dataset Development National Project

Participated in the National Information Society Agency (NIA) AI Training Dataset Construction Project to develop large-scale multimodal medical datasets for cancer AI research.
Our work focused on building integrated datasets for lung cancer prognosis prediction, malignant lymphoma prognosis prediction, and radiotherapy data for cancer patients, combining medical imaging such as CT, PET, and CBCT with clinical EMR data and, in selected tasks, digital pathology images.
These datasets were designed to support AI applications including prognosis prediction, survival analysis, lesion and organ annotation, and clinically relevant decision-support research.

Smart Hospital

Smart Hospital Clinical Workflow

Participated in Korea's 2021 Smart Hospital Leading Model Project, led by Ajou University Hospital, to develop patient-safety solutions for inpatient care.
The project focused on reducing falls and pressure ulcers among high-risk hospitalized patients by applying ICT and AI-based monitoring and integrated management systems.
Our work was aligned with the development of smart hospital solutions including AI-based high-risk patient stratification, early detection, and integrated prevention workflows for safer and more efficient clinical care.
